FAQ
Common questions
Is it really free?
Yes — completely free to use. No account required, no credit card, no limits during the soft launch period.
Is my resume data private?
Your resume text is stored only in your browser session — never saved to a database. When you close the tab, it's gone.
What file types does resume upload support?
PDF, DOCX, and TXT files are all supported. Text is extracted automatically. If auto-extraction fails, you can paste manually.
Can I import jobs directly from LinkedIn or Indeed?
Not automatically — use the Job Boards tab to open any platform, find a role, and paste the job description into the Add Job form. Takes about 30 seconds per role.
